The product and design work of Santeri Hämäläinen

Musicinfo

  • AI powered SaaS distribution and promotion platform for independent artists and record labels.
  • Design based on creative innovations of multidisciplinary team, user research and digital analytics.
  • Musicians from over 100 countries have distributed over 54,000 music tracks to global music services such as Spotify, Netease and Deezer through Musicinfo.

VRT Finland

  • A mobile app for harbor workers designed to manage and monitor harbor systems efficiently while working on-site.
  • Design based on harbor worker interviews and discussions with the client.
  • Aestethic improvements to customers previous app iterations.

Tietoevry

  • Logistics management app prototype for warehouse workers and managers.
  • Design created in close collaboration with client Product Owners and End Users.
  • Prototype was successfully used for applying funding from company board and creating an open tender for application development.

Family in Music

  • A platform for independent musicians and music industry professionals.
  • In this project I worked as a development lead and a feature owner for 6 months - I had a chance to work with and learn from world class designers.
  • Successfull implementation of social media features.

Study project - Circular economy

  • Aalto University design team project.
  • Creating life-centred user journeys for reusable food, beverage and e-commerce packaging systems.
  • Deep learning of life-centred service design and systems thinking principles.
